About Myanmar

Myanmar, also known as Burma, is a country in Southeast Asia. It is known for its rich culture, beautiful landscapes, and historical landmarks.

Things to Know About Myanmar

  • Myanmar is home to the famous Shwedagon Pagoda.
  • The official language is Burmese.
  • Myanmar has a rich history of Buddism.
  • The traditional food includes dishes like mohinga and laphet.
